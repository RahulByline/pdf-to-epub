Horses at Work

The Nez Perce Indians

were once the best horse

riders and breeders in the

United States.

Nez Perce chief

Horses have been helping

people for thousands of years.

They make people's lives easier

by doing the hard jobs.

Some horses pull wagons

and farm machinery. Some work

for the police. Some work in the

circus. Some help cowboys ride

the range.
