There are seven

million horses in the

United States.

A **stallion** is a male horse.

A female is a **mare**. Boy horse

babies are called **colts**. Girl

horse babies are called **fillies**.

All horse babies are called **foals**.

A mare nurses her foal to

feed it. She keeps her foal near

her while it is growing.

A mare also protects her foal.

If there is danger, she will kick

or bite anything that may harm

her baby.
